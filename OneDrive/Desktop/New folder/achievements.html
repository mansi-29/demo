OUR ACHIEVEMENTS

DON'T JUST FLY-SOAR!

The Team was founded in 2012 by three aeromodelling enthusiasts, Ankit Jain, Anuj Sharma and Ribhu Taneja. Since the first team of 16 members the Team has grown to a 56 member ensemble comprises of students from many different branches and years of study. The Team has clinched the top spot among Indian Teams every year since 2015 and is vying for the top spot internationally. We have expanded our horizons by competing in new competitions.This year we participated in the Boeing Aerotrix Challenge, The Vistara Hackathon and the SAE ISS Challenge.

ARCIS: 2012-'13
  • 2012-2013 :ESTABLISHED AND COMPETED AT SAE.
  • The team was headed by Ankit Jain and the aero model used a single propreller driven by an engine and a geodesic fuselage that would be suitable for competetion
  • Team Arcis competed in the SAE Aerodesign West and bagged the NASA Systems Engineering award.
  • Ranked 4th among Indian and 25th among International teams
ARCIS: 2013-'14
  • 2013-2014: NEW RULES AND CHALLENGES.
  • The team headed by Ankur Kislaya came by with an aero-model that uses a single propeller driven by a motor and a truss-structured fuselage that would be suitable for design.
    • li>>SAE mandated the use of electric motors instead of IC Engines for the first time.
  • Team took part in SAE Aerodesign East and bagged the 2nd rank among Indian and 28th rank among all teams internationally.
    • ARCIS: 2014-'15
  • 2014-2015: ASCENSION TO THE TOP SPOT.
  • The team headed by Siddhant Awasthi created an aero-model with a number of unique features like an inverted U tail, twin boom, slab sided construction and tricyclic landing gear.
  • Team competed in SAE Aerodesign West and secured 1st place among all Indian Teams, a position it has held till date.
  • The Team seized the 10th rank worldwide!
    • ARCIS: 2015-'16
  • 2015-2016 :WING TWISTS.
  • The team was headed by Akshay Kumar and after much deliberation, research and testing the team conceived an airplane that employs a tractor configuration with a semi-trussed fuselage,a wing with a geometrical twist and a U tail single boom.
  • The Team experimented with modified wings with a Geometrical Twist.
  • Team competed at SAE Aerodesign West. bagged 1st place among Indian and 25th place among International Teams.
    • ARCIS: 2016-'17
  • 2016-2017 :NEW CHALLENGES- MICRO CLASS.
  • The team was headed by Saurabh Mehta. The Regular class airplane had unique features such as custom aerofoil of fuselage design that was a combination of semi-monocoque and Slab sided design and the use of vortex generator aids in improving stall performance and increasing lift.
  • Micro class airplane had features such as a semi-trussed tractor configuration, a wing with polyhedral and an inverted T-tail.
  • Team competed in both Regular and Micro class
  • Regular class Indian rank-1, International rank 20. Micro Class Indian rank-3, International Rank 17.
    • ARCIS: 2017-'18
  • 2017-2018: Team participated in both Regular and Micro class.
  • We secured 2nd place in Micro class written design report and 3rd position in regular class oral presentation.
  • Also, earned 8th position in the world and 1st in Indian SAE.
